Pick a Cube

Description:
This program makes a cube and displays 8 copies that rotate on their own axes, aligned in a circle around a center cube. Selecting any outer cube will make the center cube copy that cube. Cube functionality is done by: translating the prototype cube to the origin, scaling and rotating at the origin, and then translating to the correct spot on the outer circle.
